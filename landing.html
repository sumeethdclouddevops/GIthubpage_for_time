📖 How to Use

For Shift Handover:

  1. Select your agent name from the dropdown
  2. Choose your current shift
  3. Fill in all handover details and task information
  4. Click "Generate Handover" to create the document
  5. Copy to clipboard and share with your team

For DC Monitoring:

  1. View real-time status of all data centers
  2. Use filters to view specific rings or business hours
  3. Check the deployment planner for scheduling maintenance
  4. Monitor countdown timers to non-business hours
